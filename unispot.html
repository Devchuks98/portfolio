About UniSpot

A comprehensive mobile platform designed to enhance student life

UniSpot is a revolutionary mobile application that bridges the gap between students across universities. It's more than just a social platform - it's a comprehensive ecosystem that helps students connect with like-minded peers, discover earning opportunities, and stay informed about campus activities.

The app creates a vibrant community where students can share experiences, trade goods, find study partners, and never miss out on important events happening around their campus.

Key Features

Everything students need in one powerful app

🛍️

Student Marketplace

Buy and sell textbooks, electronics, furniture, and other items with fellow students. Find great deals and earn money from items you no longer need.

👥

Student Peering

Connect with like-minded students who share your interests, study similar subjects, or are looking for study partners and project collaborators.

💬

Real-time Chat

Stay connected with friends and classmates through our integrated chat system. Share files, create group chats, and never miss important conversations.

📱

Social Posts

Share your thoughts, experiences, and achievements with the community through regular posts. Connect with students from different universities.

🔍

Campus Discover

Never miss out on campus events, activities, and opportunities. Discover what's happening at your university through story-like posts from fellow students.

💰

Earning Opportunities

Find part-time jobs, freelance gigs, and earning opportunities posted by students and local businesses. Turn your skills into income.
